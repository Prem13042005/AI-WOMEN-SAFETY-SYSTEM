Empowering Safety with AI

This AI-Based Women Safety and Emergency Response System is a project built by third-year Computer Science Engineering students to provide real-time protection and assistance for women.

The system includes voice-based SOS triggering, location tracking, and an AI-powered danger prediction model that assesses risk based on time, place, and context.

Our mission is to leverage technology for public safety, ensuring fast emergency responses and risk alerts with a user-friendly interface and real-time backend logic.
